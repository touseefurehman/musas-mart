Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

1. Do I need to create an account to place an order?

No, you do not need to log in or create an account. You can place an order directly without any registration.

2. How can I place an order?

You can place an order by filling out a simple form with your name, phone number, product name, address, and preferred order method.

3. What are the available order methods?

We offer two ways to place an order:

  • WhatsApp Order: You will be redirected to WhatsApp with your order details pre-filled. Just hit "send".
  • Direct Order: The order is placed directly on our website without redirection.

4. How do I track my order?

After placing the order, you will receive a unique Order ID. You can use that Order ID (or your phone number) on our Track Order page to check the current status of your order.

5. Will I get confirmation for my order?

Yes, after placing the order, you'll see a confirmation message on the screen and optionally on WhatsApp if that method is used.

6. Is it safe to order without logging in?

Yes. We keep your personal details secure and only use them to fulfill your order. No account is required, but your data is still treated with care.

7. Can I cancel or modify my order?

To cancel or modify your order, please contact us via WhatsApp or email as soon as possible using your Order ID.

8. What should I do if I lose my Order ID?

If you lose your Order ID, you can track using your phone number or contact our support team for help.

9. How long does it take to process my order?

We aim to process all orders within 24 hours. Delivery time depends on your location.
